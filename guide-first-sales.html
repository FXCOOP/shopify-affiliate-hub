1Leverage Your Existing Network

This is your fastest path to first sales. Don't be shy about asking friends and family for support.

What to Do:

  • Send personal messages (not mass emails) to 20-30 close contacts
  • Explain your new business and ask for support
  • Offer a "friends & family" discount (10-20% off)
  • Ask them to share with their networks
  • Request honest reviews after purchase

"Hey [Name]! I'm so excited to share that I just launched my online store selling [products]. As one of my closest friends, I'd love your support. I'm offering 20% off for friends & family this week with code FRIENDS20. Would mean the world if you could check it out: [link]. And if you know anyone who might like these products, I'd really appreciate you sharing! ‚ù§Ô∏è"

Expected results: 5-15 sales in first week

2Instagram Organic Growth

Instagram is still the #1 platform for ecommerce. But you need to post strategically.

The Strategy:

  • Post daily: 1 feed post + 3-5 stories
  • Content mix: 40% product showcases, 30% lifestyle/behind-scenes, 20% educational, 10% promotional
  • Use Reels: Short videos get 10x more reach than photos
  • Hashtag strategy: Use 15-20 hashtags (mix of large, medium, small)
  • Engage actively: Comment on 50 posts daily in your niche

üì± Example Reel Ideas:

  • "Unboxing my own products!" (behind-the-scenes)
  • "3 ways to style this [product]" (educational)
  • "POV: You just got your order from us" (customer experience)
  • "How I pack your orders with love" (process video)
  • "This vs That" (product comparison)

Hashtag example for jewelry store:

#jewelryshop #handmadejewelry #jewelryaddict #jewelrylover #etsyjewelry #shopsmall #jewelryoftheday #jewelrygram #jewelrydesign #bohojewelry #minimalistjewelry #jewelrystore #supportsmallbusiness #handcrafted #artisanjewelry

Expected results: 10-20 sales per month after 2-3 months of consistent posting

3TikTok Viral Strategy

TikTok has the highest organic reach of any platform. One viral video can generate hundreds of sales.

What Works on TikTok:

  • Authenticity over perfection: Raw, unedited videos perform better
  • Hook in first 2 seconds: "POV:", "Wait for it...", "This changed my life..."
  • Show, don't tell: Product in action, not just sitting there
  • Trending sounds: Use popular audio to boost reach
  • Post 2-3 times daily: Consistency is key

üé¨ Viral Video Formula:

  1. Second 0-2: Hook ("I can't believe this $20 product...")
  2. Second 3-10: Show problem ("My jewelry always gets tangled")
  3. Second 11-20: Show solution (your product solving it)
  4. Second 21-30: Results/transformation
  5. Second 30+: CTA ("Link in bio!")

Expected results: Potential for 50-200+ sales from ONE viral video (10K+ views)

4Facebook Groups (Free Traffic)

Join 20-30 Facebook groups in your niche. But DON'T spam your links!

The Right Way:

  • Provide value first: Answer questions, give advice, be helpful
  • Build relationships: Comment on others' posts regularly
  • Soft promote: "I actually sell these, happy to help!" (in context)
  • Join buy/sell groups: Some allow promotional posts on specific days
  • Share success stories: Post customer reviews (not product links)

"Oh amazing! I'll check it out. Anyone else ordered from them?"

This creates authentic social proof and discussion about your store without being spammy.

Expected results: 5-10 sales per month from groups

5Pinterest Marketing (Long-Term Traffic)

Pinterest drives consistent, long-term traffic. It's like free SEO for visual products.

Pinterest Strategy:

  • Create 20-30 pins per product: Different designs, angles, text overlays
  • Vertical images only: 1000x1500px optimal size
  • Keyword-rich descriptions: Pinterest is a search engine
  • Join group boards: Get more exposure
  • Pin daily: Use Tailwind to schedule (free plan available)

üìå Example Pin Titles:

  • "Boho Jewelry Under $30 | Handmade Necklaces"
  • "Minimalist Gold Earrings for Everyday Wear"
  • "Sustainable Jewelry Gifts for Her | Eco-Friendly"
  • "Layering Necklaces | How to Style Multiple Chains"

Expected results: 10-30 sales per month after 3-6 months (Pinterest takes time but compounds)

6Influencer Gifting (Micro-Influencers)

Stop trying to reach Kim Kardashian. Target micro-influencers (5K-50K followers) instead.

How to Find & Work with Micro-Influencers:

  1. Search hashtags: Find people posting in your niche
  2. Check engagement: 3-5% engagement rate is good
  3. DM them: Offer free product in exchange for honest review
  4. Make it easy: Send product + suggested talking points
  5. No contracts: Keep it casual for first collaboration

Expected results: 10-50 sales per influencer post (depending on their audience)

  • Duration: 7-14 days
  • Promotion: Post daily reminders, stories, Reels

8Google Shopping Ads (Paid, High ROI)

If you have a budget of $10-20/day, Google Shopping is the highest ROI paid traffic source.

Why Google Shopping Works:

  • High intent buyers: People searching actively to buy
  • Visual: Your product photo shows in search results
  • Lower CPC: Cheaper than Facebook ads
  • Easier setup: Less learning curve than Facebook

Quick Setup Guide:

  1. Connect Shopify to Google Merchant Center
  2. Set up Google Ads account
  3. Create Shopping campaign
  4. Set budget: Start with $10/day
  5. Let it run for 7 days before making changes

üí∞ Expected ROI:

Budget: $300/month ($10/day)

Expected sales: 15-30 orders

Revenue: $750-1,500 (at $50 AOV)

ROI: 2.5-5x return

9Email Marketing (Convert Visitors)

Most visitors won't buy on first visit. Capture their email and follow up!

Essential Email Sequences:

1. Welcome Series (3 emails):

  • Email 1 (immediate): Welcome + 10% discount code
  • Email 2 (day 2): Your story + why you started
  • Email 3 (day 5): Bestsellers + social proof

2. Abandoned Cart (3 emails):

  • Email 1 (1 hour): "You left something behind..."
  • Email 2 (24 hours): "Still interested?" + customer reviews
  • Email 3 (48 hours): Final reminder + 10% off

10Reddit Community Building

Reddit hates self-promotion. But if you do it right, it's goldmine traffic.

The Strategy:

  • Find relevant subreddits: Join 10-15 in your niche
  • Provide value first: Answer questions, share knowledge
  • Build karma: Post helpful content for 2-4 weeks before promoting
  • Subtle promotion: Mention your store when genuinely relevant
  • Share case studies: "I started a business selling X, here's what I learned"

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Waiting for perfect: Launch with 10-15 products, improve as you go
  2. Not collecting emails: Set up pop-up from day one
  3. Ignoring mobile: 79% of traffic is mobile - test everything on phone
  4. Being too salesy: Provide value, build relationships first
  5. Giving up too soon: Most stores take 2-4 months to gain traction
  6. Not tracking results: Use Google Analytics to see what works
  7. Spreading too thin: Master 2-3 channels before adding more
