üí∞ Pricing & Plans

How much does Shopify cost per month? +

Shopify has three main pricing plans:

  • Basic ($39/month): Perfect for new stores. Includes unlimited products, 2 staff accounts, 24/7 support.
  • Shopify ($105/month): For growing businesses. Includes 5 staff accounts, professional reports, lower credit card rates.
  • Advanced ($399/month): For high-volume stores. Includes 15 staff accounts, advanced report builder, lowest credit card fees.

What's included in ALL plans: Hosting, SSL certificate, unlimited products, unlimited bandwidth, abandoned cart recovery, discount codes, gift cards, and manual order creation.

Recommendation: 95% of new stores should start with Basic ($39/month). You can always upgrade later.

Are there any transaction fees? +

It depends on your payment method:

  • Using Shopify Payments: 0% transaction fees (you only pay credit card processing fees: 2.9% + 30¬¢ per transaction on Basic plan)
  • Using third-party payment gateway (PayPal, Stripe, etc.): 2% transaction fee + the payment gateway's fees

Example: $100 sale with Shopify Payments = $2.90 + $0.30 = $3.20 in fees (3.2%)

Example: $100 sale with PayPal = $2.00 (Shopify) + $2.90 (PayPal) + $0.30 = $5.20 in fees (5.2%)

Best practice: Use Shopify Payments to avoid the 2% transaction fee.

Can I start Shopify with no money? +

Minimum realistic budget: $150-250

Here's what you absolutely need:

  • Shopify subscription: $39/month (required)
  • Domain name: $15/year (optional but recommended)
  • Marketing budget: $100-200 (for initial traffic - ads, influencers)

If you're dropshipping: You don't need inventory upfront, which saves thousands. You only pay for products after customers pay you.

Ways to start with less money:

  • Use the 3-day free trial to set up before paying
  • Use free themes instead of paid ($0 vs $140-350)
  • Focus on free traffic (social media, SEO) instead of paid ads
  • Start with dropshipping (no inventory costs)
  • Use free apps instead of paid ones
Is there a free plan or free trial? +

Shopify offers a 3-day free trial (no credit card required).

During the trial you get:

  • Full access to all Basic plan features
  • Ability to set up your store completely
  • Test all features and apps
  • No credit card needed to start

After the trial: You'll need to choose a paid plan to keep your store live and start selling.

Pro tip: Use the free trial to fully set up your store, add products, configure settings, and test checkout. When the trial ends, you're ready to launch immediately.

üöÄ Setup & Getting Started

How long does it take to set up a Shopify store? +

Basic setup: 30 minutes to 2 hours

Complete setup (ready to sell): 1-3 days

Timeline breakdown:

  • Day 1 (2-4 hours): Sign up, choose theme, customize design, set up payment methods
  • Day 2 (4-6 hours): Add 10-15 products with photos and descriptions, set up shipping rates
  • Day 3 (2-3 hours): Add legal pages, test checkout, install essential apps, final review

You don't need to be perfect before launching. Most successful stores launch with 10-15 products and improve as they grow.

Do I need technical skills or coding knowledge? +

No coding or technical skills required!

Shopify is designed for non-technical users. Everything is drag-and-drop or fill-in-the-blank.

You can do without any code:

  • Choose and customize themes (point and click)
  • Add products (just upload photos and fill in details)
  • Set up payments (connect your bank account)
  • Configure shipping (use templates or create custom rates)
  • Install apps (one-click installation)
  • Create discount codes (automated wizard)

When you might want coding: Advanced customizations like custom checkout pages or unique features. But even then, you can hire a Shopify expert for $50-500.

What do I need to start a Shopify store? +

Essential requirements:

  • Business/Personal Email: For your Shopify account
  • Business Name & Niche: What you'll sell and to whom
  • Bank Account: To receive payments
  • Products or Suppliers: What you'll sell (your own products or dropshipping suppliers)
  • Product Photos: High-quality images (minimum 5 per product)
  • Payment Method: To pay for Shopify subscription
  • Domain Name: yourstore.com (optional but recommended - $15/year)

Helpful but not required:

  • Logo (you can create one free on Canva)
  • Business license (depends on your location - check local laws)
  • EIN/Tax ID (for U.S. - get free from IRS)

Is Shopify better than WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or Wix? +

Short answer: For most people, yes - Shopify is the best choice.

Shopify vs WooCommerce:

  • ‚úÖ Shopify wins on: Ease of use, hosting included, automatic updates, 24/7 support, security
  • ‚úÖ WooCommerce wins on: Total control, cheaper (if you're technical), unlimited customization
  • Choose Shopify if: You want easy, all-in-one solution
  • Choose WooCommerce if: You love WordPress and want complete control

Shopify vs BigCommerce:

  • ‚úÖ Shopify wins on: Easier to use, better app ecosystem, larger community, better themes
  • ‚úÖ BigCommerce wins on: More built-in features, no transaction fees, better for large catalogs
  • Choose Shopify if: You want the easiest experience with best support
  • Choose BigCommerce if: You have 500+ products or doing $50K+/month

Shopify vs Wix:

  • ‚úÖ Shopify wins on: E-commerce features, scalability, payment options, shipping tools
  • ‚úÖ Wix wins on: Website builder (better for blogs/portfolios)
  • Choose Shopify if: Your main goal is selling products online
  • Choose Wix if: You need a website with a small shop (under 20 products)
Can I sell on multiple channels (Amazon, eBay, Facebook)? +

Yes! Shopify supports multi-channel selling.

Integrated selling channels:

  • Facebook & Instagram: Sell directly through posts and stories (free app)
  • Amazon: Sync your products to Amazon marketplace
  • eBay: List Shopify products on eBay
  • Google Shopping: Appear in Google search results
  • Pinterest: Product pins with buy button
  • TikTok: TikTok Shopping integration
  • Walmart: Sell on Walmart.com

Benefits: Manage inventory in one place. When someone buys on Amazon, your Shopify inventory automatically updates. No overselling!

Best practice: Start with Shopify store + Facebook/Instagram. Add other channels once you have consistent sales.

Does Shopify provide hosting and security? +

Yes! Hosting and security are included in your monthly fee.

What's included:

  • Hosting: Your store is hosted on Shopify's servers (no separate hosting needed)
  • SSL Certificate: Free SSL (the padlock in browser) - builds customer trust
  • PCI Compliance: Level 1 PCI DSS compliant (highest security for credit cards)
  • 99.99% Uptime: Your store is almost always online
  • Unlimited Bandwidth: No extra charges even if you get millions of visitors
  • Automatic Updates: Security patches applied automatically
  • DDoS Protection: Protected against attacks
  • Automatic Backups: Your data is backed up regularly

This is HUGE: With WooCommerce or self-hosted solutions, you pay $5-50/month for hosting, manage security yourself, and deal with downtime. Shopify handles all of it.

üíº Selling & Operations

Can I dropship with Shopify? +

Yes! Shopify is the best platform for dropshipping.

Why Shopify is perfect for dropshipping:

  • Easy integration with suppliers (Oberlo, DSers, Spocket)
  • Automatic order fulfillment
  • No inventory needed
  • Start with minimal investment ($39 + ads)
  • One-click product imports from AliExpress

Popular dropshipping apps:

  • DSers: Best for AliExpress dropshipping (free)
  • Spocket: US/EU suppliers with faster shipping ($0-40/month)
  • Modalyst: Premium brands and suppliers ($0-90/month)
  • Printful: Print-on-demand for t-shirts, mugs, etc. (free)

How it works: Customer orders ‚Üí Payment goes to you ‚Üí You order from supplier (they ship to customer) ‚Üí You keep the profit

How do I get paid and receive money? +

Shopify Payments (recommended):

  • Built into Shopify (no setup needed)
  • Funds deposited to your bank account automatically
  • Payout schedule: Daily, weekly, or monthly (you choose)
  • No transaction fees (just credit card processing: 2.9% + 30¬¢)
  • Supports all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay

Alternative payment options:

  • PayPal: Funds go to PayPal account (2% Shopify fee + PayPal fees)
  • Stripe: Similar to Shopify Payments (2% Shopify fee + Stripe fees)
  • Other gateways: Authorize.net, Square, etc.

Typical timeline:

  • Customer orders ‚Üí Payment received immediately
  • Shopify holds funds for 1-3 days (fraud protection)
  • Money deposited to your bank account
  • Bank processes in 1-2 business days
  • Total time: 2-5 days from sale to bank account
What products sell best on Shopify? +

Top-selling product categories in 2025:

  • Fashion & Apparel: Clothing, jewelry, accessories - evergreen demand
  • Health & Wellness: Supplements, fitness gear, self-care - growing 25% YoY
  • Home & Garden: Decor, kitchen gadgets, organization - huge market
  • Beauty & Skincare: Cosmetics, skincare, hair products - loyal customers
  • Electronics & Accessories: Phone cases, chargers, smart home - high margins
  • Pet Products: Toys, accessories, supplies - $123B market
  • Sustainable/Eco Products: Reusable items, organic products - trending up

Characteristics of best-selling products:

  • Solves a specific problem
  • Priced between $20-80 (sweet spot for impulse buying)
  • Visually appealing (photographs well)
  • Not easily found in local stores
  • Markup potential of 2-3x cost
  • Lightweight (lower shipping costs)

Pro tip: Choose a niche you're passionate about. Your enthusiasm will show in your marketing and connect with customers.
