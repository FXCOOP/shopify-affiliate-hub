üîß Troubleshooting

Problem: "I'm not getting traffic!"

After 2 weeks:

  • ‚úÖ Normal - SEO takes 2-3 months
  • Focus on social platforms first
  • Check: Are you posting consistently?

After 1 month:

  • Review analytics: Which platforms sent traffic?
  • Double down on what works
  • Ensure all links are working
  • Check: Are you providing value or just posting links?

After 2 months:

  • Audit content quality - ask for feedback
  • Increase posting frequency if needed
  • Verify ChatGPT prompts are being used correctly
  • Ensure you're engaging, not just broadcasting

Problem: "Reddit removes my posts"

Common Reasons:

  • Too new account ‚Üí Build karma to 100+ first (2 weeks)
  • Too many links ‚Üí Post more value, fewer links (90/10 rule)
  • Spam filter ‚Üí Vary your comments, don't use templates
  • Breaking rules ‚Üí Read subreddit rules carefully

Problem: "No one engages with my content"

‚úÖ Engagement Checklist:

  • Is headline compelling? (Use numbers, curiosity)
  • Is content genuinely valuable? (Would you upvote it?)
  • Are you posting at right times?
  • Are you engaging with others first?
  • Are you in right communities?

Quick Fixes:

  • Study top posts in each platform
  • Copy their structure (not content!)
  • A/B test different headlines
  • Ask questions to encourage comments
  • Reply to every comment within 1 hour

Problem: "ChatGPT content sounds robotic"

üí° Solutions:

  • Always edit output - Add your personality
  • Add personal anecdotes - Real stories = authenticity
  • Use conversational language - Write like you talk
  • Break up paragraphs - Short sentences = easier to read
  • Remove AI phrases - No "delve", "in conclusion", "moreover"

Better prompt:

"Write this in a casual, conversational tone as if texting a friend.
Use short sentences. No corporate buzzwords. Sound like a real person."
